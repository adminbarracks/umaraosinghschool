A Century of Service

A Legacy of Charitable Education Since 1939

Umrao Singh Nursery Primary School was founded in 1939 with a noble mission: to provide high-quality English Medium education to children regardless of their economic background. Located in the historic heart of Chandni Chowk, we have stood the test of time as a pillar of community service.

Our Academic Structure

We offer a holistic English Medium curriculum designed to build a strong foundation for lifelong learning.

Foundational Years

Pre-Nursery & Nursery focus on play-based learning, social development, and basic motor skills in a nurturing environment.

  • Sensory Activities
  • Storytelling Sessions
  • Basic Phonics

Primary Discovery

KG to Class 2 introduces structured learning in English, Math, and Environmental Science through interactive methods.

  • Bilingual Support
  • Numeracy Skills
  • Creative Arts

Junior Mastery

Class 3 to 5 prepares students for senior schooling with a focus on analytical thinking and academic rigor.

  • Advanced Grammar
  • Science Labs
  • Computer Literacy
